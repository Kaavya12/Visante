About Us

VISANTE Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nations, but with a difference. It is the place where opinions are actually heard and discussed.

Our main aim is to instill proficiency in every student and encourage them to promote world peace, gloomily we cannot meet everyone in person but we are switching on to an alternative way of doing a conference , meeting other and spending and great time and spreading knowledge. We wish to redefine everyone MUN Experience by providing a 2 day conference. Therefore, on behalf of the entire team, we look forward to having us join us in our conference.
